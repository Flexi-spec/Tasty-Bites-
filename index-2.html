Our Story

Small chops made the way PH loves them — fresh, generous, every single day.

Tasty Bites started in Eliozu with one simple promise: every pack leaves our kitchen fresh, hot, and generously filled. No sitting trays, no stale chin chin — just crispy puff puff, juicy gizzard, and perfectly grilled chicken made to order.

Today we serve homes, offices, parties and events across Port Harcourt, but the promise hasn't changed: fresh ingredients, consistent taste, and portions that actually fill you up.
