How It Started

From a love of small chops to PH City's favourite snack spot

Tasty Bites was born from one simple truth — nobody in Port Harcourt could find consistently great small chops that were both generous and fresh. So we decided to make them ourselves.

Starting at 62/63 East-West Road in Eligbolo/Eliozu Extension, we set up shop with a single promise: every bite would be worth it. Fresh oil, quality ingredients, and portions that actually fill the pack.

Word spread quickly across PH — from family get-togethers in Eliozu to corporate events in GRA and birthday parties in Rumuola. Today, Tasty Bites delivers across Port Harcourt daily, with a growing reputation for the tastiest puff puff and crispiest spring rolls in the city.
